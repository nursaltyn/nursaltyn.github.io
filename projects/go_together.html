You decided to go out with a friend, or, if you are sociable enough, a group of friends. Now comes the question: where should we actually go?

You could always opt for the same place you always went to. But say, you want to explore something different, or you are new in the city. That's where the problems start.

One of your friends is vegan, but another one is craving for a juicy steak. One of your friend lives far away, another has gluten intolerance; some want to sit in a cozy quiet place, and some want it loud and upbeat... How should you find a compromise?

Our solution is GoTogether: an app that recommends 2 and more users a place to go based on each and everyone's preferences. It optimizes the recommended place in such a way that everyone can find something for themselves without spending time on debates and arguments.

Currently, it is available as a prototype on HuggingFace, and helps 2 users choose a restaurant. The database of restaurants is taken from Google Review Dataset for Vermont, USA.
