Bio

I am a M.Sc. student in Language Science and Technology at the University of Saarland in Saarbrücken. I work as a Research Assistant in the Interdisciplinary Institute for Societal Computing Group (I2SC) led by Prof. Dr. Ingmar Weber (Alexander von Humboldt Professor for AI)..

I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Translation Studies at the Al-Farabi Kazakh National University. Towards the end of my third year, I discovered the existence of Computational Linguisitcs. The idea that such a complex entity as Language can be described by numbers and vectors blew my mind. I started studying Python, Statistics, NLP and machine-learning courses myself online, and immediately after graduation, I went to pursue my Master's in Natural Language Processing.

It's been an amazing and challenging journey ever since, and I am looking forward to working on more interesting projects & acquiring new experience.
